Physical goods and food rations are accepted as donations as long as the following conditions are met:

* The Donor is willing to handle shipping to the respective ports or collection stations. This will happen only after they have discussed with our team on the nature of goods,quantity,packaging and customs in the respective country.

* The goods have a shelf life of longer than 6 months after the shipping date and and an extended 3 months shelf life after the expected collection or arrival dates.

* For goods like shoes and clothes they should be fitting to people of age 3years to 19 years. We also accept clothing and sanitary related goods for the elderly. However,it should be noted that we do not do campigns for the elderly on the time being and therefore we send the donations to organisation or other groups which focus on this area and and specific group.

* For manfuctured and packed foods,dry or not, the goods must be packed or manfuctured not more than 9 months prior to the shipping date.

*All stationary and educational materials are accepted as donations,this include materials like books,pens,physical exercise materials and more of this nature. However,the materials should be in English,Swahili,French or German considering this are the main Languages used in the geographical zone we are based and operate.
